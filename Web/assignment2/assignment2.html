Introduction

We humans are smart and creative creatures that have built and created many helpful inventions to aid the world. However we have also damaged the environment as a result of it. Despite this, Environmental Restoration is looking to reverse this problem. If you are not familiar with it, Environmental Restoration is the process of fixing environment damage that has been created by either naturally or manmade. This can be done in a number of methods, from replanting to trash cleaning. The sites below provide more information on strategies and tip as well as general information about Environmental Restoration.
